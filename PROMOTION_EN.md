#  Awesome GPT Image 2 - Curated GPT Image 2 Prompts Collection

**🔗 Repository**: https://github.com/ZeroLu/awesome-gpt-image

## Project Overview

**awesome-gpt-image** is a curated collection of the best GPT Image 2 prompts and examples sourced from the X (Twitter) community.

GPT Image 2 is OpenAI's latest image generation model with significant improvements over version 1.5:
- ✅ **Better world knowledge** - More accurate representations of real-world objects and scenes
- ✅ **Improved style understanding** - Can replicate specific art styles and game aesthetics
- ✅ **Enhanced prompt adherence** - Follows complex instructions more precisely
- ✅ **Photorealistic output** - Generates images that look like authentic photographs or screenshots

## Featured Examples

### 🎮 Game & Entertainment

**GTA 6 Style In-Game Footage**
```prompt
GTA 6 in-game footage, very detailed, very realistic. Close-up shot taken from a stationary 4k monitor.
```

**Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom Style**
```prompt
In the game Zelda totk link is in a e531 series train made by him
```

### 📷 Photorealism

**RAW iPhone Quality - Subway Station**
```prompt
Create a completely RAW quality, unprocessed, unedited image with full iPhone camera quality. A subway station in USA.
```

**YouTube Time Travel Screenshot**
```prompt
Screenshot of a YouTube video showing someone who time-traveled to the Middle Ages
```
